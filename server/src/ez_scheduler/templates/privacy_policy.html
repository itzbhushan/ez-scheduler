Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our conversational AI service to create and manage signup forms (the “Service”).

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled responsibly and transparently.

Information We Collect

When you use the Service to register for an event, we collect only the information necessary to manage signups like Name, email, phone number and details requested by the event organizer.

We do not collect sensitive personal data (e.g., government IDs, or health information). We also do not allow file or image uploads through the Service.

How We Use Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Enable event organizers to manage registrations
  • Communicate confirmations and event-related updates (if applicable)
  • Maintain the functionality and security of the Service

We do not use registration data for marketing, advertising, or AI model training.

Cookies and Authentication

We use cookies only for authentication and session management. We do not use cookies for advertising, cross-site tracking, or analytics.

Data Storage and Retention

  • All registration data is stored in a securely.
  • Data is retained indefinitely to maintain accurate event records.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your information with third parties. Registrant data is not sold, rented, or otherwise disclosed outside of the Service.

AI Transparency

  • Conversations with the AI agent are not logged or stored.
  • Registrant data is not used to train AI models.
  • AI-generated forms operate only on the event information you provide in the conversation.
